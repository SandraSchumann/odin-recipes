Chili

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans with liquid
  • 1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans with liquid
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine the beef and the onion and saute for 10 minutes, or until the meat is browned and the onion is tender.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes with juice, tomato sauce, and the two cans of beans with liquid.
  3. Add the chili powder, cayenne pepper, salt, ground black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, red pepper flakes, oregano, basil, paprika, and sugar.
  4. Stir well, cover, and reduce heat to low.
  5. Simmer for 1 to 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
